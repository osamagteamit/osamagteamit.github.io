Istanbul, Turkish İstanbul, formerly Constantinople, ancient Byzantium, largest city and principal seaport of Turkey. It was the capital of both the Byzantine Empire and the Ottoman Empire.

If we imagine briefly, Topkapı Palace, Basilica Cistern, Sultanahmet, Hagia Sophia Mosque and Archeology Museum are among the must-see places in Istanbul. If you have limited time, you can at least visit Topkapi Palace here.

tourism in turkey

Tourism in Turkey is characterized by diversity, and in the past few years it has become a global destination for tourism thanks to the ingredients it possesses to achieve this ou will rarely find another country that competes with Turkey's tourist cities with its cultural diversity, as it provides you with natural places,historical monuments, coastal resorts and ski centers.

Tourism in Turkey starts from Istanbul, as it is the first stop for tourist trips to Turkey from all over the world Also, the airports in Istanbul, whether it is Ataturk International Airport or Sabiha Gokcen International Airport, are the main airports from which flights depart to the cities and regions of Turkey surrounding Istanbul
