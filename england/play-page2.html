Religious tourism Religious tourism growing in UK Religious tourism is on the increase with a growing number of people visiting historic places, according to the Church in Wales. Although only anecdotal evidence is available, church leaders say there is a renewed interest in holy sites St Winefrides Holy Well in Flintshire reports a rise of about 6,000 visitors to 36,000 in recent years
