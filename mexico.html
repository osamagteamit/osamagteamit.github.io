best places

Mexico City is the capital of Mexico and is synonymous with the country’s Federal District.
The term Mexico City can also apply to the capital’s metropolitan
area, which includes the Federal District but extends beyond it to the west, north, and east.
It is called México in Nahuatl and Ciudad de México in Spanish.


Chichen Itza

Chichen Itza was one of the largest Maya cities and is likely one of the great mythological cities (Tulan) that he refers to in later Mesoamerican literature. It is possible that the city had a diverse population, a factor that may have contributed to the diversity of architectural styles.


San Miguel de Allende

San Miguel de Allende is a Mexican city located in the central highlands of the state of Guanajuato. The city abounds with colonial-era churches, public parks, squares, and cobbled streets lined with 16th-century mansions. Wikipedia
